IMPLEMENTATIONS

Selected implementations from Ali's PCA



Lingal

Lingal is a mobile application that aims to demolish language barriers and make communication among people all over the world much easier.

The aim of Lingal is to make communication easier without having to worry about language barriers.

Lingal was awarded a sponsor prize by Indiegogo at PennApps Spring 2014

Visit Site

Learn to Code

Learn to Code was a workshop hosted by Founders and AIS. The goal was to introduce beginners to programming so that they can start building their own projects

Participants were able to pick what they wanted to work on. Student mentors helped them set up and guide them through the initial hurdles of learning how to code

The event was attended by 45 people

Visit Site

Illini Student Storage

Illini Student Storage is the most convenient way for students at the University of Illinois to store their items over the summer in Champaign-Urbana.

Illini Student Storage offers free pick-up and delivery from several locations on campus (including most of the dorms), and provides storage for the entire summer at an affordable rate. The prices are 35-60% cheaper than all competitors.
